My Vision for ITE

Strengthening Member Engagement & Investment

ITE has done an excellent job making the organization relevant to its members. However, as we look forward to the next two decades and beyond, we need to make sure we adapt to the change in technology, as well as the direction that our industry is headed. With the increased competition with other reputable professional organizations, we need to be strategic in strengthening member engagement and demonstrating return on their investment from ITE, which will increase membership retention.

ITE will remain strong as we grow the volunteer talent pool and make them feel invested in giving their time to the Institute, and by virtue to our profession.

Innovating for a Safer, Technology-Driven Future

This includes adapting to technological changes (AI, CAV, Big Data, Video-Analytics) which impact our workflows, workforce, and how we design safer infrastructure solutions for our communities. My goal is to ensure ITE adapts to the demands that will shape the future of our profession.

One major area of opportunity is partnering with vendors offering near-miss analytics (video/radar technology) and creating an on-demand SaaS platform for members—similar to OTISS.

Global Membership Outreach, Collaboration & Engagement

  • Bilateral MOUs with engineering societies globally
  • Formation of additional Student Chapters
  • Increasing outreach for Public Agency Membership
  • Considering Corporate Membership structures
  • Collaborating with professional organizations for joint meetings

Fostering Diversity in Workforce Development

I believe elevating diversity in our workforce helps recruit and retain talent—critical to the transportation industry's success. ITE can play a vital role by offering workshops at District, Section, and Chapter levels that welcome talent from diverse backgrounds and communities.

ITE has been and will always be the professional organization of my choice, and I hope many of the current and future members of ITE feel the same way.

My Why

A Commitment to Service & Leadership

Serving as ITE International President would be the honor of my life. My leadership roles over the past two decades—both professionally and in volunteer service—have allowed me to grow and understand my strengths, improving me as a leader and individual.

ITE has shaped my career, opened doors, and allowed me to build lifelong friendships. I view ITE as a platform to create opportunities for others and to positively impact communities through smarter, safer transportation solutions.

With strong support from my family, employer, and colleagues, I am ready to dedicate myself to serving ITE's 18,000+ global members.
